Transmon qubit
==========================
The transmon qubit can be described in charge basis by the Hamiltonian

.. math::
   H=4E_\text{C}(\hat{n}-n_g)^2+\frac{1}{2}E_\text{J}\sum_n(|n\rangle\langle n+1|+\text{h.c.}),

and in phase basis by

.. math::
   H=4E_\text{C}(\hat{n}-n_g)^2+\frac{1}{2}E_\text{J}\sum_n(|n\rangle\langle n+1|+\text{h.c.}),
